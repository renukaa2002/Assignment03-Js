Basic concept of bikes

Bikes, also known as bicycles, are two-wheeled vehicles that have been an integral part of transportation and recreation for centuries. With a simple yet efficient design comprising a frame, wheels, pedals, handlebars, and a saddle, bikes offer an eco-friendly and economical mode of transportation suitable for various terrains. From leisurely rides in the park to intense mountain trails, bikes cater to a wide range of users and purposes. They promote physical fitness, environmental sustainability, and accessibility, making them a popular choice worldwide. Whether for commuting, exercising, or simply enjoying the outdoors, bikes continue to be a timeless symbol of freedom and mobility.
